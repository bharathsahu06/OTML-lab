Group Lasso Optimization

🎯 Aim

To implement Group Lasso regularization which promotes sparsity at the level of feature groups rather than individual features.

📐 Mathematical Procedure
Part A: Objective Function
\( \min_w \frac{1}{2} ||y - Xw||_2^2 + \lambda \sum_{g=1}^G \sqrt{p_g} ||w_g||_2 \)

Where \( w_g \) is the sub-vector of weights for group \( g \).
