Interior Point Method in 2D Space

🎯 Aim

To extend the logarithmic barrier Interior Point Method to a multi-variable linear objective function with multiple inequality constraints.

📐 Mathematical Procedure
Part A: Linear Objective

Minimize \( f(x, y) = x + y \) subject to \( x \ge 1, y \ge 1 \).

Part B: Barrier Formulation
\( \phi(x, y) = x + y - \mu \ln(x - 1) - \mu \ln(y - 1) \)
