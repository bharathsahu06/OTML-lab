Modeling Distributed System Staleness

🎯 Aim

To analyze the convergence properties of distributed optimization in the presence of communication delays (staleness) between workers and the parameter server.

📐 Mathematical Procedure
Part A: Stale Gradient Update
\( w_{t+1} = w_t - \eta \cdot \nabla L(w_{t-\tau}) \)

Where \( \tau \) represents the delay (number of steps) since the worker last pulled parameters from the server.
