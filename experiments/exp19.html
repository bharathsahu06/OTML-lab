Quasi-Newton Method (BFGS)

🎯 Aim

To implement the BFGS (Broyden–Fletcher–Goldfarb–Shanno) algorithm for non-linear optimization which builds an approximate Hessian inverse from gradient observations.

📐 Mathematical Procedure
Part A: Rank-2 Update

Approximate the Hessian inverse \( B_k \) iteratively to avoid the computational cost of direct second-derivative calculation.

\( s_k = x_{k+1} - x_k \); \( y_k = \nabla f_{k+1} - \nabla f_k \)
