Conjugate Gradient Method

🎯 Aim

To implement the Conjugate Gradient (CG) algorithm for solving large-scale symmetric positive-definite systems of linear equations \( Ax = b \).

📐 Mathematical Procedure
Part A: Logic

Construct a sequence of orthogonal search directions \( p_k \) that minimize the distance to the solution in each step.

\( \alpha_k = \frac{r_k^T r_k}{p_k^T A p_k} \); \( x_{k+1} = x_k + \alpha_k p_k \)
